pon	Eri, me ketiket pohn mwoahlo ahpw mahsanih, “Met I pahn onehda sapahl mehkoaros!” E ahpw pil mahsanihong ie, “Ntingihedi met, pwehki mahsen pwukat me mehlel oh likilik.”
pon	Iet ngehi, Sohn, iei ngehi me kilangehr oh rongehr mepwukat koaros. Mwurin ei kilangehr oh rongehr mepwukat, I ahpw poaridi mwohn tohnlengo pwe I en pwongih.
